ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Elevating Business Solutions

What We Do

We offer a range of services, including web applications, mobile apps, and desktop software (including Accounting and Inventory control). As a reliable business partner, we are committed to meeting clients' goals. With our experienced team, we prioritize customer satisfaction, providing training and continuous after-sales service monitoring. Our business philosophy focuses on delivering the best services, integrating new technologies, and enhancing efficiency for customer satisfaction.

Our Vision To be number 1 software provider for automotive industry
Our Mission Information technology to increase efficiency and productivity of user

Accountability The cornerstone of our approach, accountability fosters trust and transparency. Rest assured, we deliver on promises, holding ourselves to the highest standards.
Devotion Dedicated to your success, we infuse passion into task. Your goals are our mission, and we are committed to achieving them together.

Historical Milestones

1990

Our Journey Starts Here

In 1990, Greenstem Business Software Sdn.Bhd was founded with a vision to be number 1 software provider for automotive industry. Our journey began with a small team of dedicated individuals who shared a passion for IT.

1991

Dos-Based System

We are poised for success as we embark on the journey to develop a cutting-edge accounting system based on the foundation of our decades of experience with DOS technology. Our dedicated team is committed to creating an innovative solution that will redefine the way businesses manage their finances.

2000

Windows-Based System

In 2000, we made a groundbreaking transition to a Windows-based platform, unlocking the full potential of modern technology. This pivotal moment allowed us to deliver a more user-friendly, versatile, and efficient accounting solution, reaffirming our commitment to innovation and ensuring our clients benefited from the latest advancements in the field.

2020

Web-Based System

In 2023, we embarked on a transformative journey by transitioning to a web-based platform. This strategic shift allowed us to adapt to the evolving technological landscape, ensuring that our clients could access our accounting system from anywhere with an internet connection.
